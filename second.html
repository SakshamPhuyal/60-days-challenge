Edson Arantes do Nascimento(Pelé) (1940-2022)

One of the greatest footballers of all time
Pelé, born Edson Arantes do Nascimento, is widely regarded as the greatest footballer in history. He won three FIFA World Cups with Brazil and scored over 1,000 goals in his career. Known for his dazzling skill, sportsmanship, and humility, he inspired generations around the world.

Achievements:

  • Won 3 FIFA World Cups (1958, 1962, 1970) — the only player in history to do so
  • Scored over 1,000 career goals
  • Named FIFA Player of the Century
  • Served as a UNESCO Goodwill Ambassador
